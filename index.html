Let's introduce ourselves

Welcome to Obsidian Transfers & Tours, your premier choice for luxury transport and tours in Crete. Our company, led by Vasilis Patelaros, offers a wide range of luxurious vehicles to suit every need: from spacious minibuses and minivans to comfortable taxi cars for more personal trips. Every vehicle in our fleet meets the highest standards of quality and luxury, ensuring an unforgettable travel experience.At Obsidian, our commitment to excellent service is evident in every journey. Whether it's a tour of Crete's historical sites or a quick transfer to the airport, our drivers are trained to provide a safe, comfortable, and enjoyable travel experience. Explore the beauty of Crete in style and comfort, choosing Obsidian for your transportation needs.Discover the difference with Obsidian and let your journey become an unforgettable experience.
