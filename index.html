About us

Let's introduce ourselves

Blacklane transfers provides Transfers & Tours with a private driver to personal and business clients in Crete Island. We assist you while you are in Crete for your business or holiday trip offering a safe, reliable & comfort Minivan, including a Chauffeur Security Services . Our Chauffeur Service is addressed to you by professional drivers sharing the same goal, providing secure and safe first-class services at competitive prices, helping our customers throughout their visit to Crete. Most noteworthy, as owners of the Minivan, we guarantee the perfect condition of the car, as well as the best rates avoiding any brokerage fees. Our specialized knowledge of local points of interest on Crete island gives you the advantage of having a personal assistant and unique concierge services. Consequently, we ensure you enjoy the most of your time in Crete. We can arrange VIP Transfers from Crete Airport, Chauffeur Service and Luxury Transfer service. Additionally, we can offer you packages from 6 hours to 24-hour driver service, tailored to your needs, in order to offer you your personal driver in combination with a luxury vehicle of your choice. We will pick you up from the airport or ports of Crete at your arrival time, upon request, as well as from any location all over Crete. Enjoy your holidays in Crete, in comfort and safety, renting a Minivan with an experienced personal driver at your disposal. We focus on the demand for high-quality, professional transportation in Crete offering the finest chauffeur service available in Crete..
